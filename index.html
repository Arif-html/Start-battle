1. Use W, A, S, D to control the spacecraft, press Space to launch。 2. The initial fuel value is 15, and it decreases by 1 point per second. When the fuel value is 0, the game ends。 3. Touching a dropped fuel bottle can increase 15 points, the maximum is 30 points。 4. Hit the enemy ship to increase 5 points. The planet needs to be hit twice, adding 10 points. 10 points deducted for hitting a friendly。 5. Lose 15 fuel points when hitting an enemy, 10 points deducted from hitting a friendly。 6. The game allows negative points。 7. Press P to pause the game, press M to mute。 Start your Star Wars!
